About Me

I was born on October 25, 2004, in Kumasi, Ghana, to my wonderful parents, Linda Antwi-Donkor and Desmond Oppong. As a proud Fante, I hold my cultural roots close to my heart. Music has always been a significant part of my life, and I love playing the piano during my free time, as well as watching inspirational videos.I am a dedicated Christian with a strong passion for my education. I completed my junior high at Solomon Bennett Memorial and continued to St. Monica's Senior High for my secondary education. Currently, I’m pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Computer Engineering at Kwame Nkrumah University of Science and Technology (KNUST).Throughout my journey, I’ve taken on various leadership roles, such as serving as the head girl in junior high school and briefly leading as the publicity head for Glinax. My interest in AI and deep learning reflects my innovative mindset and passion for advancing in cutting-edge technology.Motivated and goal-oriented, I strive for both personal and professional growth and am committed to making a meaningful impact in my field. I look forward to the exciting opportunities the future holds!
